Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-01

1. Overview

Device Information values your privacy. The App does not require account registration or sign-in.

2. What information may be accessed

To display device diagnostics, the App may read the following data locally on your device:

  • Device and system details: brand, model, Android version, CPU, display specs, sensors;
  • Network details: connectivity status, Wi-Fi SSID, BSSID, IP, DNS, and link properties;
  • Battery and thermal details: level, voltage, current, temperature, and health status;
  • Storage and memory details: storage capacity, memory usage, and system stats;
  • Installed app details: app name, package name, version, signature digests, declared permissions, and components.

3. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions for the corresponding features:

  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE and ACCESS_WIFI_STATE for network and Wi-Fi status;
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION to read current Wi-Fi SSID on Android 10+;
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES to enumerate installed apps and show app details;
  • ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION (Android 10+) to read step counter sensors and display them in the App.

4. Data transmission

All above data is processed locally on your device only and is never uploaded or transmitted to any server.

5. Third-party pages

The "Feedback" entry opens Google Forms. Any information you submit there is processed by Google under Google's privacy policy.

6. Policy updates

If app features change and affect privacy practices, this policy will be updated inside the App.
